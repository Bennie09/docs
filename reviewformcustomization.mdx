## Branding Options

You can customize the visual style of your form to match your brand.

### Available options:
- Upload your business logo  
- Change primary and background colors  
- Choose light or dark theme  
- Adjust button styles  
- Preview changes in real time  

<Tip>Make sure your branding matches your website or store colors for a consistent experience.</Tip>

---

## Editing Questions

Your form comes with a default set of questions:

- Star rating  
- Written review  
- Optional customer details  

You can add custom questions based on what your business needs.

### Supported question types:
- Text input  
- Star rating  
- Select options  
- Yes or No  
- Optional or required fields  

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Add a new question">
    1. Click Add Question  
    2. Choose the question type  
    3. Write your question  
    4. Choose if it should be required  
    5. Save to apply
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="pen" title="Edit an existing question">
    Click on any question to change text, type, or its required status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="trash" title="Delete a question">
    Press the delete icon next to the question you no longer need.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customer Details Options

You can ask for extra details from the customer if needed.

### Optional fields include:
- Customer name  
- Customer email  
- Contact number  
- Service or product purchased  

These fields can be optional or required depending on your workflow.

<Tip>Keep customer details optional to increase review completion rates.</Tip>

---

## Thank You Screen

After submitting a review, customers see a final message.

You can customize:
- Title text  
- Message content

This helps you guide customers to the next step after leaving a review.
