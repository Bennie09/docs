## Your Public Wall of Love

The Wall of Love is a public page that displays all your approved customer reviews in one place.\
AskReview generates this page automatically and keeps it updated as new reviews come in.

## What Is the Wall of Love

The Wall of Love is a dedicated page hosted by AskReview.\
It shows:

- Your business name
- Your logo (if added)
- Total reviews
- Average star rating
- All approved reviews
- Featured reviews at the top
- A clean and modern layout

This page can be viewed by anyone and is perfect for sharing as proof of your customer satisfaction.
