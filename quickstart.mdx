## Get started in three steps

Get your AskReview account ready, customize your review form, and begin collecting reviews.

---

### Step 1: Create your account and set up your workspace

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Create your AskReview account">
    Signing up takes a few seconds. You only need your email and password.

    After creating your account, you will get:
    - A personal dashboard
    - A default review form
    - Your unique review link
    - A QR code for real world review collection

    If you already have an account, just log in to continue.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="pen-to-square" title="Customize your review form">
    1. Go to the Review Form page in your dashboard  
    2. Upload your logo  
    3. Edit your questions  
    4. Adjust your colors and branding  

    <Tip>Your form updates instantly as you make changes.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

### Step 2: Share your review link

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="share-nodes" title="Send your review link to customers">
    You can share your link through:
    - WhatsApp
    - Email
    - SMS
    - Social media
    - Your website

    Your link works on any device and loads instantly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="qrcode" title="Use your QR code">
    You can download your QR code and place it:
    - On your counter
    - On receipts
    - On flyers
    - On your shop window
    - In your office

    <Tip>QR codes usually give the highest review conversion rates.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

### Step 3: Manage and display your reviews

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="folder-open" title="View and manage reviews in your dashboard">
    As reviews come in, you can:
    - Approve or archive them
    - Pin important reviews
    - Delete unwanted ones
    - Export them for marketing
    - Filter by rating or date

    Your dashboard updates in real time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="heart" title="Show your reviews to the world">
    AskReview helps you showcase testimonials using:
    - Website widgets
    - Carousels and grids
    - Inline embed components
    - Your public Wall of Love page

    <Tip>Displaying reviews can significantly increase conversions.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
