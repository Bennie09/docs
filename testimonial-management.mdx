## Pinned Reviews

Pinning a review keeps it at the top of your:
- Wall of Love  
- Widget displays  
- Grid layouts  

Pinned reviews are useful for:
- Showing your strongest testimonials  
- Highlighting reviews with detailed praise  
- Featuring reviews from important customers  

---

## Deleted Reviews

AskReview allows deletion only for **manually submitted reviews**.

You can delete:
- Spam  
- Fake submissions  
- Duplicate reviews  
- Test reviews  

### Important
Reviews collected through **automated review request emails** cannot be deleted.  
These reviews are:
- Auto approved  
- Trusted by default  
- Protected from deletion  

This ensures authenticity and prevents manipulation of automated feedback.

---

## Automatic Review Trust System

Reviews that come from:
- Automated emails  
- Official review request messages  

Are marked as **trusted** and:
- Cannot be deleted  
- Cannot be modified  
- Are auto approved  

This protects the integrity of your business ratings.

---

## Filtering and Sorting Reviews

You can filter reviews by:
- Star rating  
- Date  
- Pinned status  

Sorting helps you quickly find the reviews you want to promote or feature.

---

## Exporting Reviews

You can export approved reviews for:
- Social media posts  
- Website updates  
- Marketing assets  
- Business presentations  

Exports include all important information like rating, text, and date.
