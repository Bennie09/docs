## Display Your Testimonials Anywhere

AskReview gives you multiple widgets for showcasing reviews on any website.  
Each widget can be customized and embedded with a simple code snippet.

<Card title="Customize a widget" icon="code" horizontal href="#">
  Open the Widgets page in your dashboard, choose a widget, customize it, and copy the embed code.
</Card>

---

## How To Access and Use Widgets

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion icon="window-restore" title="Step 1: Open the Widgets page">
    Go to your AskReview dashboard and click the Widgets tab.  
    You will see all available widgets listed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="sliders" title="Step 2: Select a widget">
    Click on the widget you want to use.  
    This opens the customization page for that specific widget.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="palette" title="Step 3: Customize your widget">
    You can adjust:
    - Colors  
    - Layout  
    - Animation  
    - Display settings  
    - Number of reviews  
    - Review sorting  
    - Widget size  

    <Tip>Your preview updates instantly as you customize.</Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="copy" title="Step 4: Copy the embed code">
    Click the Copy button under the preview to copy the widget script.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="paste" title="Step 5: Paste into your website">
    Paste the embed code into:
    - HTML  
    - React components  
    - Webflow embed blocks  
    - WordPress  
    - Wix  
    - Framer  
    - Shopify  

    The widget will appear immediately.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Badge Widget

The badge widget shows:
- Average rating  
- Total approved reviews  

Good for:
- Navigation bars  
- Footers  
- Headers  

---

## Grid Widget

The grid widget displays multiple reviews together in a clean layout.

Good for:
- Testimonial sections  
- About pages  

---

## Inline Widget

The inline widget displays a single featured testimonial inside your text.

Good for:
- Blogs  
- Product pages  

---

## Popup Widget

The popup widget shows reviews inside a floating modal.

Good for:
- Landing pages  
- Checkout pages  

---

## Carousel Widget

The carousel rotates through multiple testimonials.

Good for:
- Homepages  
- Hero sections  

---

## Wall of Love Widget

This is an embeddable version of the full testimonials layout.  
It shows a large list of your best reviews directly on your website.

Good for:
- Dedicated testimonial sections  
- Portfolio sites  
- Service pages  
- Long scroll landing pages  

This is not the same as your Wall of Love public page.  
Instead, this widget allows you to embed a full testimonials layout directly into your own website.
